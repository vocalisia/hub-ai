# AI Ecosystem in Montreal

Montreal is home to **Mila** (the world's largest academic AI lab),
making it a global hub for AI research and startups.

## Why Montreal?

- **Mila** : led by Yoshua Bengio, one of the godfathers of deep learning
- **Strong talent pool** : top universities (UdeM, McGill, Polytechnique)
- **Government support** : generous tax credits for AI R&D
- **Bilingual advantage** : French and English ecosystem

## Key Players

1. **Element AI** (acquired by ServiceNow)
2. **Scale AI** supercluster
3. **IVADO Labs** for industry AI solutions
4. **Hundreds of startups** across all sectors
