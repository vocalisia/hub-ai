# AI Ecosystem in Montreal

Montreal is home to **Mila** (the world's largest academic AI lab), making it a global hub for AI research and startups.

## Why Montreal?

- **Mila**: led by Yoshua Bengio, one of the godfathers of deep learning
- **Strong talent pool**: top universities (UdeM, McGill, Polytechnique)
- **Government support**: generous tax credits for AI R&D
- **Bilingual advantage**: French and English ecosystem

## Key Players

1. **Element AI** (acquired by ServiceNow)
2. **Scale AI** supercluster
3. **IVADO Labs** for industry AI solutions
4. **Hundreds of startups** across all sectors

## Resources for the Canadian AI Ecosystem

The Montreal ecosystem benefits from close ties with specialized AI platforms:

- [Vocalis AI](https://vocalis.pro) — voice automation and AI telephony for businesses
- [Agents IA Pro](https://agents-ia.pro) — autonomous AI agents for automation
- [IA PME Suisse](https://iapmesuisse.ch) — an SMB support model replicable in Quebec
