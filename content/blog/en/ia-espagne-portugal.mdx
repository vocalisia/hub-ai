# AI in Spain and Portugal 2025 — Madrid, Barcelona, Lisbon

The Iberian Peninsula is undergoing a full-scale **digital transformation**. Spain and Portugal, long considered technology followers, are now asserting themselves as serious AI players on the European stage. With competitive costs, a growing talent pool and a bustling startup ecosystem, Madrid, Barcelona and Lisbon are attracting investors and entrepreneurs from around the world.

## Spain: A Rapid Rise

### The ENIA National Strategy

Spain launched its **Estrategia Nacional de Inteligencia Artificial (ENIA)** with an investment of 600 million euros over the 2021-2025 period. This strategy is built around six pillars:

1. **Strengthening scientific research** in AI
2. **Promoting digital capabilities** of businesses
3. **Developing sectoral data platforms**
4. **Integrating AI into industrial value chains**
5. **Training talent** and adapting the labor market
6. **Establishing an ethical framework** for AI

By 2025, the results are tangible: Spain now ranks in the **European top 10** for AI scientific publications and in the global top 15 for the number of AI startups.

## Barcelona: The Mediterranean AI Hub

Barcelona has established itself as the **technology capital of Southern Europe**. The Catalan city combines economic strengths, quality of life and an innovation ecosystem to attract the best talent.

### Barcelona's Startup Ecosystem

The **22@** district, a former industrial quarter converted into an innovation district, houses more than 1,500 tech companies. Among Barcelona's notable AI startups:

- **Typeform** — AI-based intelligent conversational forms
- **Factorial** — AI-augmented HR and talent management
- **Verse** — Smart payments and fintech
- **Kognia Sports Intelligence** — AI-powered sports video analysis

### Mobile World Congress and AI

The **MWC**, the world's largest mobile phone trade show held annually in Barcelona, has become a must-attend event for AI. In 2025, over 40% of exhibitors showcased solutions integrating artificial intelligence, from smart 5G to advanced voice assistants.

Companies looking to stay informed about these AI developments can follow the in-depth analyses published on [Vocalis Blog](https://vocalis.blog), which regularly covers conversational AI and automation trends.

### Universities and Research

Barcelona boasts an exceptional university network for AI:

- **UPC (Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya)** with its BSC supercomputing center
- **UPF** and its Department of Information and Communication Technologies
- **ICREA** which attracts world-class researchers

The **Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC)** hosts MareNostrum, one of Europe's most powerful supercomputers, crucial for training advanced AI models.

## Madrid: The Power of Large Corporations

The Spanish capital concentrates the **headquarters of major companies** investing heavily in AI.

### Madrid's Champions

- **Telefonica**: The telecom giant deploys AI in its networks and services through its AURA division
- **BBVA**: The pioneering bank uses machine learning for fraud detection and customer personalization
- **Indra**: The technology company applies AI to defense, transportation and energy
- **Repsol**: The oil group uses AI to optimize refining and accelerate the energy transition

### Madrid Content City and Creative AI

The **Madrid Content City** complex has become a hub for AI applied to media and entertainment. Production studios use AI for post-production, automated dubbing and AI-generated content creation.

## Portugal: Lisbon, the Rising Star

### The Portuguese Tech Miracle

Portugal has undergone a **remarkable transformation** in less than a decade. Lisbon has evolved from a tourist destination to a **major European tech hub**.

### Web Summit and the Catalyst Effect

The arrival of the **Web Summit** in Lisbon in 2016 was a major accelerator. The event attracts 70,000 participants annually and has put Portugal on the global tech map. In 2025, AI is the dominant theme of the conference.

### Lisbon's AI Ecosystem

Lisbon offers unique competitive advantages:

- **Operating costs** 30 to 40% lower than Paris or London
- **NHR tax regime** attractive for qualified foreign professionals
- **Language proficiency** — Portuguese people are fluent in English
- **Quality of life** that attracts international tech talent

Startups like **Unbabel** (AI translation), **Feedzai** (AI fraud detection) and **Sword Health** (AI-powered rehabilitation) illustrate Portugal's dynamism.

### Porto and Industrial AI

Portugal's second city specializes in **AI applied to the textile, cork and agri-food industries**. The University of Porto and INESC TEC are recognized research centers in computer vision and robotics.

## Iberian Sectors of Excellence

### Tourism and AI

Tourism, the economic pillar of the peninsula, is being transformed by AI:

- **Dynamic hotel and airline pricing**
- **Multilingual chatbots** for tourist reception
- **Personalized recommendations** based on visitor behavior
- **Crowd forecasting** to optimize the management of tourist sites

### Agrotech and AI

Iberian agriculture is introducing AI for:

- **Smart irrigation** in arid regions
- **Crop disease detection** by drone and computer vision
- **Harvest optimization** through advanced weather forecasting
