# AI in Switzerland 2025

Switzerland has become one of the **world leaders in artificial intelligence**,
with Geneva and Zurich as epicenters of innovation.

## Why Swiss SMEs Are Adopting AI

Swiss SMEs represent more than 99% of the country's economic fabric. AI adoption enables them to:

- **Automate repetitive tasks**: invoicing, customer support, inventory management
- **Improve decision-making**: predictive analytics, intelligent dashboards
- **Reduce operational costs**: up to 40% savings on certain processes

## Recommended Tools for Swiss SMEs

1. **ChatGPT / Claude** for content generation and support
2. **Zapier / Make** for workflow automation
3. **Custom solutions** tailored to the specific needs of each business

## Resources for Swiss SMEs

Swiss companies have access to a rich ecosystem to support their AI transformation:

- [IA PME Suisse](https://iapmesuisse.ch) — dedicated support for Swiss SMEs
- [Vocalis AI](https://vocalis.pro) — voice automation and AI telephony
- [Agents IA Pro](https://agents-ia.pro) — ready-to-deploy AI agents for businesses
